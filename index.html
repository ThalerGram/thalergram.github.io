Our Vision

We are working to build our own network and a private, end-to-end encrypted messaging application. The goal is to design a communication platform that does **not** require phone numbers, email addresses, or personal data for registration. We aim to remove metadata exposure (such as sender/receiver identities, IP addresses, and transaction details) from the network wherever possible, so users can communicate freely and securely while retaining full control over their data and coins.
