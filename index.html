Burn & Scarcity

A controlled, transparent burn mechanism will gradually remove tokens from circulation. Each burn event will be verifiable on the Polygon blockchain. The aim is to mirror Bitcoin’s DNA — predictable issuance and deliberate scarcity — while evolving toward a more resilient economic design.

Future Blockchain & Token Migration

TalerGram is initially a Polygon token. We are developing a dedicated blockchain tailored to private, censorship-resistant communication and transactions.

When the new TalerGram chain launches, every holder of the Polygon-based token will receive 1 native coin per token held (1:1 migration). Early holders keep full parity.

Migration events and verifications will be announced publicly on @ThalerGram and the official Session community.

Vision

We will build a private, peer-to-peer messaging system that minimizes metadata and resists censorship. No phone numbers. No email. No central authority. End-to-end encryption by default. Minimal identifiable data on the network. Users keep custody of keys and coins. Predictable monetary policy. Intentional scarcity.
